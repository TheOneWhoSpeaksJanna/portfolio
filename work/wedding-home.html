How we work

Four steps, no mystery

A simple path from "we're engaged" to "we're married." You always know what's happening.

Chat

Free 30-min call. We talk budget, vibe, and whether we're a fit. No pitch deck.

Plan

Moodboard, timeline, and a working budget in a shared doc you can edit any time.

Book

We lock venues and suppliers. You approve each one — we don't book on assumption.

Run

On the day, we're the ones with the clipboard. You're the one with the champagne.

Real weddings

The Todmorden Mill wedding

A converted cotton mill, 70 guests, and a couple who wanted folk music and proper Yorkshire pudding. We fought the venue's sound limit so the ceilidh actually happened. Rain held off until 9pm — we'd booked the marquee anyway, just in case.

What they said they'd have struggled with: the supplier contracts and the running order. That's the part we did.

Straight talk — what we're not

  • We don't do destination weddings abroad. We're Yorkshire-based and that's where we're good.
  • We're not the cheapest. If budget is the only factor, a month-of coordinator elsewhere will cost less.
  • We won't oversell a "luxury" package you don't need. Most couples are fine with Partial.
  • We can't guarantee the weather. We can guarantee a plan B that doesn't feel like a backup.

No fake five-star reviews, no "120 weddings planned" claim — ask us directly and we'll tell you honestly how many we've done and show you the photos.
